Mastermind

Rules

A combination of 4 colored pegs is generated randomly at the beginning of the game.


The user must guess the combination generated by placing pegs on the board. In this case, you have to click the buttons to change their color.

The combination must be guessed in ten turns, else the game is over.


When the guess is submitted, hints appear on the right side of the board.

Pegs are placed randomly in the square :

- a white peg indicates that a peg has the right color but the wrong position.

- a red peg indicates that one of the pegs has both the right color and the right position.
